THE ARTEMIS MISSION
Artemis Summary:
The Artemis mission is sending the first woman and first person of color to the Moon using innovative technologies.
The mission is exploring the Moon for scientific discovery, technological advancement, and learning how to live on another world. The Lunar Gateway is a space station that will orbit the Moon and support the Artemis mission. It is planned to be built no sooner than 2025.
Artemis Missions:
1. Artemis I - Launched on November 16, 2022. It was an uncrewed flight test of the Space Launch System (SLS) and the Orion spacecraft around the Moon.
2. Artemis II - Expected to launch around September 2025. This will be the first crewed flight test of the SLS and Orion. It aims to orbit and study the Moon.
3. Artemis III - Expected to launch in September 2026. It will land the first humans near the lunar South Pole.
4. Artemis IV - Expected to launch in September 2028. A more powerful version of the SLS will deliver parts of the Lunar Gateway.
5. Artemis V - Expected to launch in March 2030. Will deliver two parts of the Lunar Gateway and send four astronauts to it.
6. Artemis VI - Expected to launch in March 2031. Will connect the Crew and Science Airlock to the Lunar Gateway.
